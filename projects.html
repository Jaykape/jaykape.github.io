  • Analytic Stacks
    • This project is an attempt to collects related information to keep on track to the new theory of analytic spaces introduced by Peter Scholze and Dustin Clausen. Their lecture series "Analytic Stacks" at IHES.

     Lecture Notes on Analytic Geometry by Scholze (joint w/ Clausen)
     This is a quote from Scholze's Notes
     "What do we mean by analytic geometry? We mean a version of algebraic geometry that
     (1) instead of merely allowing polynomial rings as its basic building blocks, allows rings of convergent power series as basic building blocks
     (2) instead of being able to define open subsets only by the nonvanishing of functions, one can define open subsets by asking that a function is small, say less than 1
     (3) strictly generalizes algebraic geometry in the sense that the category of schemes, the theory of quasicoherent sheaves over them, etc., all embed fully faithfully into the corresponding analytic category."
